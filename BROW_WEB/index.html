About

The app will ask the user for an input regarding the issue she/he is facing (safety, funding, education). according to the number of people who have raised an issue in the community we will generate a heat map. This will let the authorities know which particular place is facing which issue and can also help in organising government funds for particular areas.
This app will hence let us empower different stratas of the society and also help in raising particular issues if necessary.

What problems do women face?


  • Education

    Education is a powerful force which should reach all the individuals. But in many areas women are denied proper education.

  • Funding

    In today's scenario it is clearly evident that women are getting fundings way lesser than men. This difference in pay scale is found commonly in various industries and across various cities.

  • Safety

    In a country like India, women harassment cases are ever growing. Women might not feel safe in offices, market places or even in their own localities. There might be a lot of unsafe places abhout which women don't know all across the country.

How can we solve the problems?


According to the input given by the user, the application will generate clusters according to the proximity of other previous inputs. Heat Maps will be formed with the clusters as the basis. These Heat Maps can be used for viewing various problems the country is facing in diverse parts.

Who can use the app?

  • Women all around the country
  • NGOs looking forwared to provide support
  • Venture Capitalists interested in funding women based entrepreneurs.
